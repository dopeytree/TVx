- ğŸ“Š **Scanlines** - Horizontal scan pattern authentic to analog television technology
- ğŸŒ‘ **Vignette** - Edge darkening with smooth feathering simulates natural light falloff on curved glass screens
- ğŸ’ **Edge Glass Mirage** - Vaseline-like blur on frame edges recreates the thick glass tube effect
- ğŸ¯ **Frame Edge Blur** - Controllable anti-aliasing creates the soft focus characteristic of vintage displays
